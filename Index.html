How It Works

Route. Learn. Improve.

RouteIQ operates as a closed-loop intelligence system. Every transaction makes it smarter.

01
⚡

Instant Decision

Payment arrives. RouteIQ evaluates all available PSPs in <100ms using hierarchical bandit scores, economic reward modeling, and Q-Learning state.

02
🔁

RL-Powered Retry

If the first PSP fails, reinforcement learning takes over. It has learned from thousands of failure sequences which PSP to try next based on failure type and country. 83.8% of failed payments get recovered.

03
🧠

Continuous Learning

Every outcome — success or failure — immediately updates 3 levels of intelligence: local (country), regional (Africa/APAC/EU), and global. Knowledge transfers across markets instantly.

Intelligence Architecture

Two layers of adaptive intelligence

Bandits for selection. Reinforcement learning for recovery. Each layer solving a different problem.

🏗️

Hierarchical Bandits

Learns simultaneously at local (country + method), regional (Africa/APAC/EU/Americas), and global levels. When Nigeria learns Fincra is best, Kenya benefits immediately. Solves cold-start for new markets.

🎯

Thompson Sampling + UCB

Combines probabilistic exploration (Thompson Sampling) with mathematically optimal exploration bonuses (Upper Confidence Bound). PSPs that haven't been tested enough get automatically explored.

♟️

Q-Learning (RL)

Reinforcement learning layer that optimizes long-term reward sequences, not just immediate outcomes. RouteIQ thinks ahead — keeping backup PSPs warm so they're ready when the primary fails.

💰

Economic Reward Optimization

Optimizes profit, not just success rate. Every decision weighs revenue, PSP cost, latency penalty, and failure cost simultaneously. Routes to maximize margin, not just approvals.

🛡️

Circuit Breaker

Automatically disables PSPs when failure rate exceeds 85% over last 20 transactions. Self-heals when PSP recovers. Zero human intervention required. Protects revenue 24/7.

📡

Drift Detection

Detects gradual PSP degradation before it becomes catastrophic. Alerts within 20 transactions when a PSP drops 20%+ below baseline. Early warning system for ops teams.
